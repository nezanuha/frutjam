Card components are versatile containers for combining related information into a single, visually cohesive unit. With support for images, titles, descriptions, and action buttons, cards organize content hierarchically and adapt to various layouts. The Frutjam card system offers multiple color variants and styles for product showcases, blog posts, testimonials, and dashboard elements.
